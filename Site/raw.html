About Raw

Monday Night Raw has been WWE's flagship program since its debut on January 11, 1993. For over three decades, Raw has delivered non-stop action, compelling storylines, and unforgettable moments featuring the greatest superstars in professional wrestling.

Raw airs live every Monday night, bringing three hours of excitement to millions of fans worldwide. The show features championship matches, intense rivalries, and spectacular entertainment that has made WWE a global phenomenon.
