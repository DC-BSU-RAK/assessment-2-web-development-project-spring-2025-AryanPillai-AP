About SmackDown

Friday Night SmackDown has been a cornerstone of WWE programming since 1999. Known as "The Blue Brand," SmackDown has delivered incredible matches, compelling storylines, and unforgettable moments for over two decades.

SmackDown airs live every Friday night, bringing two hours of high-flying action and entertainment to fans worldwide. The show features some of WWE's most popular superstars competing for championship gold and glory.
